In Plain Sight follows the story of a Federal Marshal with the Witness Protection program who must hide her high-risk, high-impact job from her family.  To those that know her, Mary Shannon is a glorified meter maid, but her real job is much more dangerous.  She must oversee federal witnesses who have been relocated through the Witness Protection program and make sure that they stay safe.The series stars Mary McCormack in the title role and Lesley Ann Warren as her mother, Jinx. (Working title - Mary Sunshine)In Plain Sight is produced by Universal Media Studios for USA Network.
