A reversioning of the 1970s show, BBC One presents a three-part tale titled Upstairs, Downstairs.

It picks up almost 5 years after the original series ended, in London's Belgravia in 1936. The show follows a wealthy family and their servants as they deal with the changes of a pre-war society.
