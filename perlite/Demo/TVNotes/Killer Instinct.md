Killer Instinct details the chilling stories behind the bizzare and deviant crimes cimmited by San Francisco's most aberrant criminals and the detectives whose job it is to stop them. The Series centres around Detective Jack Hale of the SFPD's deviant crime unit. Hale has chosen to be on the front line of the city's toughest division after taking a hiatus from the force following the death of his partner, whom he had a personal relationship with, in the line of duty. Intense and private, Hale is haunted by his own personal demons and fights to save his sanity and career.
