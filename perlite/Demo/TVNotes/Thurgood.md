HBO presents a live performance of the solo show, Thurgood, written by George Stevens, Jr. and performed by Laurence Fishburne. In it, Fishburne depicts the life and times of the first African-American Supreme Court Justice, Thurgood -- from his childhood roots to his work as a Civil Rights attorney and his time on the bench.
