Anna Wetherell (Eve Hewson) travels to New Zealand to start a new life during the gold rush in 1866 where she meets Emery Staines (Himesh Patel) and Lydia Wells (Eva Green) in this six-part series based on Eleanor Catton's book of the same name.

[Premiered originally in New Zealand's TVNZ 1 on 17 May 2020, in the UK on BBC One on 21 June 2020 and in the US in Feb 2021]
