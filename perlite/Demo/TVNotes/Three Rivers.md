This is a medical drama about an acclaimed transplant hospital and the team, which hold life and death in their hands everyday.  The team is comprised of Dr. Andy Yablonski, the lead surgeon; Dr. Miranda Foster, a woman trying to live up to her father's reputation; Dr. David Lee, a resident more interested in his private affairs; Ryan Abbott, the new transplant coordinator struggling to balance the aspects of his job; Pam Acosta, the surgical nurse and Dr. Sophia Jordan, the head of surgery who needs to keep everyone in line.   Every case is viewed from the perspective of the team, the recipient and the donor.

This show is set in Pittsburgh at Three Rivers Medical Center, William H Foster Transplant Wing.
