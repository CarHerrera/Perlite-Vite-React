How to Get the Guy is an hour-long romantic reality show following the lives of four young San Francisco women looking for love. Each woman meets one of these molds: The Dreamer, The Girl Next Door, The Party Girl and The Career Girl.

The show comes from the producers of Queer Eye For The Straight Guy.
