Glenn Martin, DDS is a stop-motion series created by Eric Fogel and Michael Eisner and animated in Canada by Cuppa Coffee Studios. It involves Glenn Martin, a traveling dentist who takes his family on a cross-country road trip to protest the demolition of his favorite childhood park. The trip brings the family together and also brings some stories that will be passed down forever.
