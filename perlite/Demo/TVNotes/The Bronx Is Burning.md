A limited run series about the Yankees' 1977 World Series attempt,
based on the book "Ladies and Gentlemen, The Bronx is Burning" by
Jonathan Mahler. Premiering in 2007, the production is being offered on
ESPN, with episodes directed by TV veteran Jeremiah Chechnik, and a cast
that includes John Turturro, Oliver Platt, Max Casella, Christopher Mac
Donald, and other TV vets in multiple episodes, and a host of others in
cameo roles. A co-production of ESPN Original Entertainment, East Coast
Films, and Tollin/Robbins Productions, this continues to broaden the type of
entertainment provided by the sports channel.
