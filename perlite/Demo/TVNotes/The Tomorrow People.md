Based on the 1970s British show, Stephen Jameson (Robbie Amell) discovers his sleepwalking is really a result of teleporting in his sleep. He is a part of the Tommorrow People that includes John, Cara and Russell, who share his unique abilities such as telekinesis, teleportation, and telepathy. However, a paramilitary group know as Ultra, led by Dr. Jedikiah Price is hunting people like them.
