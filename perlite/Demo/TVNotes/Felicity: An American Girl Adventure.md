Felicity Merriman, the fourth character in American Girl's popular line of historical dolls and books, is the central character in this heartwarming family drama set in the time leading up to the American Revolution .  Ten-year-old Felicity resides with her Patriot family in Colonial Williamsburg, Virginia, during the year of 1775, where she befriends Elizabeth, a young girl from a Loyalist family.  In the passing year, Felicity embarks on many adventures that test both her loyalty and courage revolving around friends, family and the coming revolution.
