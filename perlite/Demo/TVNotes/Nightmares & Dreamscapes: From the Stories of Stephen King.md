Featuring episodes adapted from stories in the book of the same name, as well as some from other works by Stephen King, Nightmares and Dreamscapes is an eight-episode anthology miniseries that originally aired on TNT in summer 2006. William Hurt, William H. Macy, and Tom Berenger are just a few of the stars who appear in the macabre tales about evil toys, dead rock stars, and parallel dimensions.
