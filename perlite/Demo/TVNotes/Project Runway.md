Contestants are judged on the clothing they create in a number of challenges for a chance to create a collection for New York Fashion Week and win money to start their own fashion line, an editorial feature in a magazine as well as other prizes in the fashion designing competitive reality show.

First aired on Bravo with host Heidi Klum, Tim Gunn as the designer's mentor and Nina Garcia and Michael Kors as the main judges. It moved to Lifetime beginning with its sixth season. Zac Posen took over from Kors beginning with the 11th season. 

The show moved back to Bravo beginning with the 17th season with new host Karlie Kloss replacing Heidi Klum, Runway alum Christian Siriano took over as mentor. Brandon Maxwell and former editor-in-chief of Teen Vogue Elaine Welteroth joined Nina Garcia on the judging panel. Karlie Kloss left as host after the 18th season. 

The show moves to FreeForm for its 21st season with Heidi Klum returning as host, Christian Siriano as mentor, and Law Roach joining Nina Garcia on the judging panel.
